Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Lotus IoT cost?

A complete Lotus IoT station costs approximately 1.5-1.8 million VND, which is 90-95% cheaper than commercial monitoring systems. We also offer a subscription service at 100,000 VND/month that includes cloud storage, maintenance, and support.

Is technical knowledge required to use the system?

No technical expertise is needed! Our mobile app is designed to be user-friendly with simple controls and visual indicators. We also provide training and support during installation.

How long does the battery last?

The system is powered by solar energy with battery backup, ensuring 24/7 operation. The battery can run for 2-3 days without sunlight, making it reliable even during cloudy weather.

Can I monitor multiple ponds?

Yes! You can connect multiple Lotus IoT stations to a single app account and monitor all your ponds from one interface. Each station operates independently while sharing data to the central dashboard.

What happens if internet connection is lost?

The system stores data locally and will automatically sync once the connection is restored. Emergency alerts can still be sent via GSM module (phone calls) even without internet.

Is the system weatherproof?

Absolutely! The station is designed with waterproof housing (IP65 rating) to withstand rain, humidity, and harsh weather conditions typical in the Mekong Delta.

Do you offer installation and training?

Yes! We provide complete installation services and hands-on training to ensure you're comfortable using the system. Our support team is also available for ongoing assistance.

Can this system be used for other crops?

Yes! While designed for lotus cultivation, the system can be adapted for rice fields, aquaculture (shrimp-lotus), hydroponic vegetables, and other water-dependent crops. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
