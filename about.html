About Lotus IoT

Preserving tradition through innovation

Our Mission

Lotus IoT aims to develop an intelligent monitoring system for lotus ponds - a plant of high livelihood and cultural value in the Mekong Delta that is suffering from climate change, saltwater intrusion, and water source changes.

We believe that technology should serve the people, especially farmers who are the backbone of our agricultural heritage. By providing affordable, accessible IoT solutions, we help preserve traditional livelihoods while preparing for future challenges.

Our Goal

Protect livelihoods, reduce environmental risks, and contribute to sustainable agriculture in the Mekong Delta

The Challenge We Address

Climate Change Impact

The Mekong Delta is one of three regions globally most affected by climate change. Over 60% of lotus cultivation areas in Dong Thap Muoi suffer from saltwater intrusion and drought.

Economic Loss

Annual losses reach approximately 120 billion VND due to environmental damage, with farmers lacking scientific monitoring tools.

Manual Monitoring

Most farmers still monitor pH, salinity, and water levels manually, relying on experience and observation, leading to slow responses.

High Cost Barriers

Existing commercial monitoring stations cost 25-40 million VND, making them inaccessible to small-scale farmers.

Our Solution

Lotus IoT bridges the technology gap with an affordable, user-friendly, and locally adapted solution

01

Integrated Multi-Sensor System

5 sensor types (water level, pH, salinity, temperature, light) integrated on a compact 30x30cm floating station, powered by 10W solar panel with 18650 battery backup for 24/7 operation.

  • Cost: < 2 million VND/station
  • 90-95% cheaper than commercial alternatives
  • Data collection every 5 minutes via WiFi/4G
02

Smart Data Analysis & Early Warning

Anomaly detection using Z-score and moving average algorithms to identify unstable trends (e.g., rapid pH decrease or water level drop within 2 hours).

  • Automatic alerts when thresholds exceeded
  • Relay-controlled water pump activation
  • Emergency phone calls via SIM800L module
  • 24-48 hours earlier decision-making
03

AI-Powered Predictions

Machine Learning models (Linear & Random Forest Regression) for predicting water level changes, drought risk conditions, and lotus blooming periods.

  • 24-hour water level forecasting
  • Drought risk assessment
  • Bloom time predictions
  • Multi-pond environmental mapping
04

User-Centered Control System

Unlike traditional monitoring systems, Lotus IoT provides two-way interaction with farmers through an intuitive mobile application.

  • Remote pump control from smartphone
  • Customizable alert thresholds
  • Visual dashboard with color-coded warnings
  • Direct phone calls for emergencies
